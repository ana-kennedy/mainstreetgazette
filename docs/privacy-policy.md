# Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026

Main Street Gazette is a local-first feed reader and podcast player for Disney-related news, videos, and podcasts.

## Information We Collect

Main Street Gazette does not require an account and does not collect personal information from users. The app does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, or in-app purchases.

## Information Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

- Feed cache
- Saved item IDs
- Enabled and disabled source preferences
- App settings
- Feed checkpoint date
- Podcast queue
- Podcast playback progress

This information is used only to provide app functionality and is not sent to Main Street Gazette servers.

## Network Requests

When you refresh feeds or play podcast episodes, the app connects to enabled third-party source providers to retrieve RSS feeds, YouTube feed data, article links, images, and podcast audio. Those providers may receive standard network information, such as your IP address, device/browser user agent, and request details, according to their own privacy policies.

Main Street Gazette does not sell, share, or use this information for advertising or tracking.

## Third-Party Services

The app can fetch content from third-party publishers and platforms listed in the source catalog. These sources are controlled by their respective providers. Opening an article, video, or external link may take you to a third-party website or app, where that provider's privacy policy applies.

## Children's Privacy

Main Street Gazette does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it by uninstalling the app. You can also remove saved items, change source selections, and update settings inside the app.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as the app changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
