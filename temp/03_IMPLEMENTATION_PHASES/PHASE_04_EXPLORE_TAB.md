# Phase 04 — Explore Disney and Park Dashboards

## Goal
Make Explore destination-led, with useful live park resources and a small amount of emotional atmosphere.

## Root structure
- Title: `Explore Disney`.
- Prompt: `Where would you like to go today?`
- Destination cards for Walt Disney World, Disneyland Resort, Disney Cruise Line, international resorts, and Disney entertainment where supported.
- Do not use infinite mixed-content scrolling on the root.

## Park dashboard groups
1. Today at the Park
2. Plan Your Day
3. Today's Gazette
4. A Little Park Magic

## Trust labels
- Live
- Updated recently
- Estimated
- Planning guide
- Official information
- Independent data
- Main Street Gazette estimate

Every dynamic card shows last update time and offers `About This Information`.

## Realistic data
Prioritize weather, park hours, attraction waits/status, entertainment schedules, refurbishments, virtual queues when available, official notices, event dates, and Gazette coverage.
Transportation times/status appear only when a reliable supported source exists. Otherwise show route guidance and published service interruptions, clearly labeled as planning guidance.

## Radio
Start with external links to approved independent stations. Do not embed raw streams without written permission. Label stations as independent and provide acknowledgements.
