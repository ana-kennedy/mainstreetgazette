# Phase 09 — Startup Wizard: The Gazette Arrival

## Goal
Welcome first, explain clearly, and defer nonessential permissions.

## Pages
1. Welcome to Main Street Gazette — `Begin Today's Edition`
2. A Newspaper for Disney Fans — brief explanation of mixed content
3. My Magic — destinations and interests, optional and clearly described
4. The Living Gazette Library — explains that usefulness grows over time
5. Save the Magic — explains Library and collections
6. Gazette Alerts — explains choices but does not force system permission
7. Make the Gazette Yours — system accessibility, sounds, motion, contrast
8. Meet Your Gazette — News, Explore, For You
9. Your First Edition Is Ready — `Open Today's Gazette`

Final optional action: `Take the Grand Tour`.

Every feature name receives a short description. No unexplained jargon.
