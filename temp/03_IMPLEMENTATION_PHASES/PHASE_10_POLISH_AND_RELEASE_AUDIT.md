# Phase 10 — Visual, Sound, Accessibility, Performance, and Release Audit

- Wire only approved sounds and remove missing/broken references.
- Test all screens with VoiceOver, braille display/simulator, switch/keyboard navigation, Dynamic Type, Bold Text, Button Shapes, Increase Contrast, Reduce Transparency, Reduce Motion, grayscale, dark mode, and low bandwidth/offline conditions.
- Verify focus restoration and two-finger scrub on every pushed screen/modal.
- Verify no decorative icon is announced.
- Verify all icon-only controls have labels, roles, states, and hints where needed.
- Verify adaptive sections do not create blank headings.
- Verify stale data is never presented as live.
- Verify user-owned content survives optimization and migration.
- Verify notification permission denial recovery.
- Verify TypeScript and launch validation.
