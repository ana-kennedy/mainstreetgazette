# For You

## Header
Warm time-of-day greeting, followed by no more than three major headings.

## Continue Your Adventure
Mixed content resume queue. Show only genuinely resumable content.

## The Gazette Library
Show a few pinned/recent collections and a clear `Open the Gazette Library` action. Do not dump hundreds of saved items onto the root page.

## Today's Picks
A small, diverse set based on My Magic and available content. Hide when recommendations are weak.

## Trip Companion
Compact optional card. No trip means an invitation to add one, not a permanent empty dashboard.
