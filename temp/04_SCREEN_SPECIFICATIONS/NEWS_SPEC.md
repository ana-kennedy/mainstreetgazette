# News — Today's Gazette

## Header order
1. Optional greeting
2. Today's Gazette, heading level 1
3. Date and compact edition summary
4. Settings gear
5. Current edition/view button
6. Search the Gazette
7. Optional Editor's Note
8. Headlines

## View menu descriptions
- **Today's Magic** — A thoughtfully organized edition of meaningful recent Disney coverage.
- **My Magic** — Highlights destinations and topics selected by the guest.
- **All Headlines** — The complete chronological list of available content.

## Card spoken example
`Disney announces a new nighttime parade. Article from Disney Parks Blog. Two hours ago. About five minutes. Double tap to read.`

Do not announce decorative thumbnails or media icons. Expose the meaningful media type as text/state.
