# Your Gazette — Complete Settings Specification

## 1. Your Experience
Intro: `Make the Gazette feel just the way you like it.`

- **My Magic** — Choose destinations and topics the Gazette should highlight.
  - Destinations: Walt Disney World, Disneyland Resort, Disney Cruise Line, International Parks, Disney Entertainment.
  - Interests: Attractions, Dining, Festivals & Seasonal Events, Resorts, Merchandise, runDisney, Disney Vacation Club, Accessibility, Disney History, Official Announcements, Community Highlights.
  - Media type is not an interest filter; articles, podcasts, and videos remain ways to experience a topic.
- **Gazette Alerts** — Choose when the Gazette may gently let you know something is ready or timely.
- **Reading Experience** — System/light/dark, text override only if needed, standard/spacious/compact cards, full/reduced/text-first artwork, Gazette Reader behavior, seasonal touches, motion, sound.
- **Accessibility** — VoiceOver enhancements, braille-efficient layout, high-contrast cards, always-visible labels, simplified navigation. Defaults must follow system settings without requiring a special accessibility mode.
