# Visual Design System

## Mood
A calm Main Street reading room: elegant newspaper structure, generous white space, subtle warmth, and restrained seasonal details.

## Color
Use one primary Gazette accent with semantic system colors for success, warning, error, and information. Do not assign loud permanent colors to every content type. Type labels may use subtle outlines and icons plus text.

## Cards
Shared radius, padding, border, and elevation. Prefer thin rules and contrast over heavy shadows. Cards must reflow rather than clip.

## Seasonal touches
Optional, small, and never content-obscuring. Hide from accessibility. Respect reduced artwork and Reduce Motion.

## Arrival motion
Short fade/translate only when allowed. Do not stagger focusable content in a way that delays accessibility or causes focus movement.

## Icons
Visible landmarks for sighted and low-vision users. Decorative by default. Meaning must always exist as text or accessible state.
