# Master Acceptance Checklist

## Navigation
- Exactly three bottom tabs.
- Gear opens Your Gazette from every root tab.
- No duplicate Settings tab.

## News
- One view button with exactly Today's Magic, My Magic, All Headlines.
- Saved content does not appear as a News view.
- Search and position memory work.

## Reader
- Full clean text shown when permitted and available.
- Summary-only state is explicit.
- Original source is always credited and reachable.
- Podcasts/videos/social receive appropriate standalone pages.

## Explore
- Data provenance and freshness visible.
- No fake live transport or crowd claims.
- Webcams excluded.
- Radio starts as external approved links.

## For You and Library
- Large libraries remain navigable through Library/search/collections.
- Initial save never forces a collection choice.
- Collections support mixed media.
- User content survives optimization.

## Alerts and trips
- Descriptions explain every alert.
- Denied notification permission can be recovered through iPhone Settings.
- Trip start/end fields and multiple trips work.
- Reminder rules are versioned and destination-aware.

## Accessibility
- Heading navigation on every major screen.
- No decorative icon announcements.
- Combined cards and actions are concise.
- Maximum Dynamic Type, dark mode, high contrast, Reduce Motion pass.
- Braille labels are concise and stable.

## Performance/storage
- Backfill is resumable.
- No media downloads during broad history retrieval.
- Low-power/low-storage conditions pause work.
- Adaptive sections hide weak content.

## Quality
- `npm run typecheck` passes.
- No missing sound imports.
- No user-visible technical copy remains outside developer mode.
